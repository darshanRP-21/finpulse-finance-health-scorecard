# 💰 FinPulse — Personal Finance Health Scorecard > An AI-powered personal finance health scoring tool that evaluates your financial situation across 5 key pillars and generates personalised advice. 🔗 **[Try the Live Demo](https://darshanrp-21.github.io/finpulse-finance-health-scorecard/)** --- ## 🧾 About the Project Most people have no idea where they actually stand financially. I built **FinPulse** to change that — enter 8 simple numbers and get a detailed Finance Health Score out of 100, a visual breakdown across 5 financial pillars, and an AI-generated personalised action plan. This project applies personal finance concepts I've been studying — savings benchmarks, debt-to-income ratios, liquidity coverage — and brings them to life through code and AI. --- ## 📊 Features - ✅ Finance Health Score out of 100 - ✅ 5-pillar breakdown with animated progress bars - ✅ Colour-coded scoring (green / amber / red) - ✅ AI-generated personalised financial guidance - ✅ Animated score dial - ✅ Input validation & error handling - ✅ Fully responsive design --- ## 🧠 Finance Concepts Applied | Pillar | Benchmark Used | |---|---| | Savings Rate | ≥ 20% of monthly income | | Debt Load | Debt-to-Income ratio < 30% | | Emergency Fund | 3–6 months of expenses | | Investment Ratio | ≥ 10% of monthly income | | Spending Discipline | Expense ratio + insurance coverage | --- ## 🛠️ Built With - Python - HTML - Claude AI API (Anthropic) --- ## 📸 Preview > Enter your monthly income, savings, expenses, debt, emergency fund, investments and insurance → get your score instantly. --- ## ⚠️ Disclaimer This is an **educational project only** and does not constitute financial advice. All scoring is based on general personal finance benchmarks.
